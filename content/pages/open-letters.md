Open Letters are respectful, well-researched public letters to technology companies and organizations.

The goal is not outrage.

The goal is constructive pressure.

Each Open Letter tries to:

- identify a real user problem
- explain why it matters
- make a clear request
- suggest a practical path forward
- invite thoughtful public discussion

Some Open Letters may be paired with a detailed White Paper when the topic needs deeper technical or business analysis.
