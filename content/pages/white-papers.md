White Papers are deeper research-backed documents that support selected Open Letters.

An Open Letter explains the issue publicly.

A White Paper studies the topic in more detail.

A White Paper may include:

- technical background
- user personas
- business reasoning
- implementation paths
- risks and trade-offs
- recommendations

Not every Open Letter needs a White Paper.

But when a topic deserves depth, the White Paper becomes the detailed companion document.
